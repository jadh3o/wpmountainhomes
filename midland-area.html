About the Downtown & Midland Area

The Downtown and Midland Avenue corridor is where Woodland Park's community life happens. This walkable area features locally owned shops, cozy restaurants, antique stores, and gathering places that give the city its small-town mountain character. Living here means being at the center of it all — the farmers market, holiday festivals, First Fridays, and the daily rhythm of a town that knows its neighbors.

What's Here

The Midland Avenue corridor features Woodland Park's most beloved local businesses — Hungry Bear for all-day breakfast, Fiesta Mexicana for authentic Mexican food, Cafe Leo for artisan coffee. Antique shops and boutiques line the cabin-like buildings along the main street. The upcoming Tava House Development will add a culinary school, event center, and coffee shop to the downtown mix.

Memorial Park, with its tennis courts, playgrounds, and winter ice skating pond, is within walking distance. The Dinosaur Resource Center provides a unique family attraction.

Real Estate

Properties in the downtown area range from charming older homes and cottages to condos and townhomes. This is one of the more affordable entry points into Woodland Park real estate, though premium properties with updated finishes and mountain views command higher prices. Some commercial/residential mixed-use properties are also available.

The Vibe

Downtown Woodland Park has a personality — it's casual, friendly, and unpretentious. People wave from their porches, dogs are everywhere, and conversations happen naturally at the coffee shop or post office. If you want to feel part of a community, not just live in a house, the Midland area delivers.
