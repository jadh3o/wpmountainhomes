Woodland Heights

A central Woodland Park neighborhood within walking distance of downtown shops, restaurants, and the heart of the community.

About Woodland Heights

Woodland Heights occupies a prime central position in Woodland Park, putting residents within easy walking distance of the downtown commercial district along Midland Avenue and Highway 24. This is one of the most convenient locations in the city — close to restaurants, shops, City Market, and community events.

Location & Lifestyle

Living in Woodland Heights means you can walk to Cafe Leo for a morning coffee, grab lunch at Joanie's Deli, and still be home in time for a sunset deck session overlooking the mountains. The neighborhood's central location also means easy access to the annual farmers market, holiday festivals, and community events that make Woodland Park special.

The Tava House Development, the city's anticipated multi-use project near downtown featuring a culinary school, event center, and coffee shop, is nearby — potentially increasing the area's walkability and appeal even further.

Homes

Homes in Woodland Heights range from mid-century cottages to updated mountain homes. The housing stock is diverse — you'll find cozy 2-bedroom starter homes alongside larger family residences. Many properties have been renovated over the years while maintaining their mountain character.

Ideal For

Woodland Heights is perfect for buyers who prioritize walkability and convenience. It's popular with retirees who want to stay active in the community, young professionals, and anyone who values being at the center of Woodland Park life.
