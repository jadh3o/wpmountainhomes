About the Shop

A Taste of Norway in the Mountains

Cafe Leo is not your typical mountain-town coffee stop. Located at 320 W Midland Ave in Woodland Park, it is a Norwegian-owned cafe and bakery that brings genuine European warmth to the heart of the Pikes Peak region. With a 4.6-star rating across more than 1,020 reviews, it has become one of the most popular and most-reviewed gathering spots in town -- and for good reason.

The cafe is open from 6 AM to 8 PM daily, making it one of the earliest and latest options in Woodland Park. Whether you are stopping in for a pre-dawn coffee on the way to a hike or settling in for an evening pastry and tea, Cafe Leo has you covered. The seating area offers mountain views and a European cafe atmosphere that feels relaxed, unhurried, and genuinely different from anything else in town.

What Makes It Special

Why Locals Love Cafe Leo

Authentic Norwegian Sweet Buns

The Norwegian ownership isn't just a footnote -- it shapes the entire experience. The authentic Norwegian Sweet Buns are made from scratch using traditional recipes and you genuinely cannot find them anywhere else in Colorado. Get there early; they sell out.

Spicy Avo Bagel & Pesto Turkey

The sandwich menu has developed its own following. The spicy avo bagel and the pesto turkey are the two most frequently recommended items -- flavorful, fresh, and made to order.

Huge Tea & Coffee Menu

The coffee and tea selection goes well beyond the typical coffee-shop afterthought. Classic espresso drinks, specialty teas, and seasonal creations cover every taste.

Fresh Pastries Baked Daily

The pastry case rotates with fresh-baked items every day. From croissants to specialty cakes, everything is made in-house and pairs perfectly with a morning coffee or afternoon tea.

Mountain Views from Your Seat

The seating area faces the mountains, and on a clear day the views are stunning. It's the kind of backdrop that makes a simple coffee and pastry feel like a European holiday.

European Cafe Vibes

The interior leans into a clean, bright European aesthetic -- designed to make you linger. It's the kind of place where people come for a quick coffee and end up staying for an hour.
