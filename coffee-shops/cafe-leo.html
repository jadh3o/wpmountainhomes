About Cafe Leo

Cafe Leo is not your typical mountain-town coffee stop. Located at 320 W Midland Ave in Woodland Park, it is a Norwegian-owned cafe and bakery that brings genuine European warmth to the heart of the Pikes Peak region. With a 4.6-star rating across more than 1,020 reviews, it has become one of the most popular and most-reviewed gathering spots in town -- and for good reason.

The cafe is open from 6 AM to 8 PM daily, making it one of the earliest and latest options in Woodland Park. Whether you are stopping in for a pre-dawn coffee on the way to a hike or settling in for an evening pastry and tea, Cafe Leo has you covered. The seating area offers mountain views and a European cafe atmosphere that feels relaxed, unhurried, and genuinely different from anything else in town.

What Makes It Special

The Norwegian ownership is not just a footnote -- it shapes the entire experience. Cafe Leo serves authentic Norwegian Sweet Buns that you genuinely cannot find anywhere else in Colorado. These are made from scratch using traditional recipes, and regulars know to get there early because they sell out. If you have ever traveled to Scandinavia and missed the bakeries, this is the closest thing you will find on the Front Range.

Beyond the sweet buns, the menu is wide and thoughtful. The coffee and tea selection is huge, covering everything from classic espresso drinks to specialty teas that go well beyond the typical coffee-shop afterthought. The pastry case rotates with fresh-baked items daily, and the sandwich menu has developed its own following. The spicy avo bagel and the pesto turkey sandwich are the two most frequently recommended items -- flavorful, fresh, and made to order.

The atmosphere adds to it. The seating area faces the mountains, and the interior leans into a European cafe aesthetic: clean, bright, and designed to make you linger. It is the kind of place where people come in for a quick coffee and end up staying for an hour.

Address & Directions

Cafe Leo is located at 320 W Midland Ave, Woodland Park, CO 80863, on the west end of the main downtown corridor. Coming from Highway 24, head south on Baldwin Street and continue west on Midland Ave. The cafe is on the right with street parking available along Midland and in nearby lots. It is an easy walk from most downtown shops and neighborhoods.

Living Near Cafe Leo

The west end of Midland Avenue puts you close to some of Woodland Park's most desirable neighborhoods. Areas like Tamarac, Paradise Estates, and the western reaches of the Midland corridor offer larger lots, mature trees, and a quieter feel while still being a short walk or drive to downtown amenities. The mix of newer construction and established mountain homes means there are options at multiple price points.

Homes in these neighborhoods typically range from the low $400s to the high $600s, with some custom builds and larger-lot properties reaching above $700,000. For buyers who want easy access to great food, mountain views, and the daily rhythm of a small mountain town, living near Cafe Leo checks a lot of boxes.
