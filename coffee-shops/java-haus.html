About Java Haus

Java Haus is one of Woodland Park's highest-rated coffee shops and a genuine community gathering place. Located at 116 W Midland Ave in the heart of downtown, it has earned a 4.8-star rating across 219 reviews for good reason: the coffee is excellent, the food is homemade, and the atmosphere is the kind of welcoming, no-pressure environment that keeps people coming back every morning.

What sets Java Haus apart from a typical coffee stop is the space itself. There is tons of indoor seating spread across multiple areas, including a separate meeting room that local groups, book clubs, and small businesses use regularly. The laid-back vibe makes it equally suited for catching up with a friend, working remotely on your laptop, or just reading the paper with a mocha. Free parking is available right next door, so there is never a hassle getting in and out.

What Makes It Special

Java Haus is the kind of coffee shop that becomes part of your routine. Regulars know the baristas by name, and the baristas know their orders. The espresso drinks are consistently well-made -- the lattes and mochas are the most popular -- and the seasonal specials keep things interesting throughout the year.

But the food is a big part of the draw too. The blueberry muffins are baked fresh and disappear fast. The cheese danishes have a loyal following. Combined with a solid coffee menu, it adds up to a morning destination that feels more like a neighborhood kitchen than a coffee counter.

The separate meeting room is a feature you will not find at most local shops. It gives Java Haus a dual purpose: casual hangout on one side, productive workspace on the other. Remote workers appreciate the reliable Wi-Fi and the fact that nobody rushes you out. Group gatherings, from HOA meetings to birthday parties, happen here regularly.

Address & Directions

Java Haus is located at 116 W Midland Ave, Woodland Park, CO 80863, right on the main downtown strip. There is free parking in the lot next door. Coming from Highway 24, turn south onto Baldwin Street and the shop is within a block on your left. It is easy to combine a coffee stop with errands at nearby downtown businesses.
