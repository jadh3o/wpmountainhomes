About the Shop

Woodland Park's Favorite Coffee Spot

Java Haus is one of Woodland Park's highest-rated coffee shops and a genuine community gathering place. Located at 116 W Midland Ave in the heart of downtown, it has earned a 4.8-star rating across 219 reviews for good reason: the coffee is excellent, the food is homemade, and the atmosphere is the kind of welcoming, no-pressure environment that keeps people coming back every morning.

What sets Java Haus apart from a typical coffee stop is the space itself. There is tons of indoor seating spread across multiple areas, including a separate meeting room that local groups, book clubs, and small businesses use regularly. The laid-back vibe makes it equally suited for catching up with a friend, working remotely on your laptop, or just reading the paper with a mocha.

What Makes It Special

Why Locals Love Java Haus

Lattes and Mochas Done Right

The espresso drinks are consistently well-made -- the lattes and mochas are the most popular orders. Baristas know their craft, and regulars know them by name.

Blueberry Muffins & Cheese Danishes

The blueberry muffins are baked fresh and disappear fast. The cheese danishes have their own loyal following. Combined with great coffee, they make a complete morning destination.

The Meeting Room

A separate meeting room gives Java Haus a dual purpose: casual hangout on one side, productive workspace on the other. HOA meetings, book clubs, and birthday parties happen here regularly.

Built for Remote Work

Tons of indoor seating, reliable Wi-Fi, and a relaxed atmosphere where nobody rushes you out. Remote workers love this spot for long productive mornings.

Free Parking Next Door

No circling the block, no meters. The free parking lot right next door makes getting in and out completely hassle-free.

The Laid-Back Vibe

Java Haus is the kind of place where you come for coffee and stay for the atmosphere. Unhurried, friendly, and perfectly suited for laptops, books, or long conversations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Java Haus FAQ

Does Java Haus have good WiFi for remote work?

Yes. Java Haus is one of the most popular remote work spots in Woodland Park, CO. They have free WiFi, tons of indoor seating, and a relaxed atmosphere where you can work for hours without being rushed.

Can I reserve the meeting room at Java Haus?

Java Haus has a separate meeting room used regularly by local groups, book clubs, and small businesses. Contact them directly at the shop for availability and reservations.

What are the most popular items at Java Haus?

The lattes and mochas are the most ordered drinks. For food, the blueberry muffins and cheese danishes are local favorites that sell out regularly.

Is there parking at Java Haus in Woodland Park?

Yes, there is a free parking lot right next door. Street parking is also available along Midland Avenue.
