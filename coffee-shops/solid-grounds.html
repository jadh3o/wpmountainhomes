Solid Grounds Coffee Roasters

One of the highest-rated coffee shops in Woodland Park, CO -- a cozy mountain cabin where the coffee cake actually tastes like coffee and the Americano has hints of berries.

★★★★★ 4.9 rating · 91 reviews
Coffee Shop in Woodland Park, CO

Mountain Cabin Vibes, Seriously Good Coffee

Solid Grounds Coffee Roasters isn't just another coffee shop in Woodland Park, CO -- it's an experience. Tucked into a charming mountain cabin at 108 N Park Street, this local roaster has earned a devoted following and an almost-perfect 4.9-star rating. From the moment you walk in, the live edge wood ceilings, crackling fireplace, and warm lighting make you feel like you've stepped into your favorite mountain retreat.

What sets Solid Grounds apart is the care that goes into everything they serve. They roast their own beans on-site, and you can taste the difference. The medium-roast Americano has subtle hints of berries that keep regulars coming back morning after morning. And their chai? Fabulous doesn't begin to cover it.

What Makes It Special

Why Locals Love Solid Grounds

The Mountain Cabin Atmosphere

Live edge wood ceilings, a cozy fireplace, and varied seating options -- from cushy armchairs to communal tables -- make this feel more like a friend's cabin than a coffee shop.

Breakfast Burritos That Steal the Show

The breakfast burritos at Solid Grounds are legendary among locals. Hearty, flavorful, and the perfect companion to a morning coffee. Many regulars come for the burrito as much as the brew.

Homemade Coffee Cake (That Tastes Like Coffee)

Most coffee cakes have nothing to do with coffee. This one actually does. It's homemade, rich, and has a genuine coffee flavor that pairs perfectly with their medium roast.

Berry-Noted Medium Roast Americano

Their signature medium-roast Americano has subtle berry undertones that make it stand apart from any chain coffee. It's the kind of cup that converts people who say they don't like black coffee.

The Chai Is Fabulous

Not a coffee drinker? The chai at Solid Grounds is a destination order in its own right -- warm, perfectly spiced, and made with the same care they put into their roasts.

On-Site Roasting

Solid Grounds roasts their own beans, which means everything is fresh. You can often smell the roasting process when you walk in, and you can buy bags to take home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Solid Grounds FAQ

What is Solid Grounds Coffee Roasters known for?

Solid Grounds is known for its mountain cabin atmosphere with live edge wood ceilings and a cozy fireplace, amazing breakfast burritos, homemade coffee cake that actually tastes like coffee, and a medium-roast Americano with hints of berries. They roast all beans on-site.

Does Solid Grounds roast their own coffee beans?

Yes. Solid Grounds roasts all their beans on-site in Woodland Park. You can often smell the roasting process when you walk in, and they sell bags of freshly roasted beans to take home.

What are the hours for Solid Grounds?

Monday through Saturday from 7AM to 5PM, and Sundays from 8AM to 5PM.

Is Solid Grounds good for remote work?

Yes. The varied seating options, cozy fireplace, and relaxed cabin atmosphere make it a comfortable and productive spot for remote work in Woodland Park, CO.
