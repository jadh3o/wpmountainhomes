Model Citizen Coffee

Woodland Park's artisan coffee roaster and tasting room, bringing European-style espresso, daily coffee flights, and a true passion for the craft to Midland Avenue.

About Model Citizen Coffee

Model Citizen Coffee is not just another coffee shop. Opened by Enrique and Tabitha, who developed their roasting expertise in Hawaii, this is a craft roastery and tasting room that approaches coffee with the same care and intention that a winemaker brings to a vineyard. They source, roast, and serve every bean themselves, and the result is some of the best espresso you will find anywhere along the Front Range.

The space on West Midland Avenue is designed to feel like a European coffee house: warm, inviting, high-end without being pretentious. It is the kind of place where the owners know your name, the conversation runs deep, and you leave feeling like you learned something new about the coffee you have been drinking your entire life.

What Makes It Special

The coffee flights are the signature experience at Model Citizen. Each day the shop offers three different coffees for side-by-side tasting, allowing you to explore how origin, altitude, and roast profile affect flavor. It is educational, fun, and genuinely eye-opening, even for experienced coffee drinkers. Reviewers consistently describe it as one of the most enriching coffee experiences they have ever had.

The espresso program is exceptional. Shots are pulled with precision, and the decaf is so well-roasted that customers say they cannot tell it apart from the caffeinated version. Tabitha also bakes butter cookies and gluten-free treats in house, and the shop carries a selection of premium teas including a standout jasmine blend.

Model Citizen also offers coffee classes and roasting education for anyone who wants to go deeper into the craft. It is not just a place to buy coffee; it is a place to understand it.

What to Try

  • Daily coffee flights with three rotating single-origin selections
  • European-style espresso and cappuccinos
  • The Nat/Nat blend (half decaf, remarkably smooth)
  • Tabitha's house-baked butter cookies and gluten-free treats
  • Jasmine tea and specialty tea selections
  • Fresh-roasted beans to take home by the bag

Living Near Model Citizen Coffee

Model Citizen sits in the heart of Woodland Park's downtown corridor, surrounded by neighborhoods that offer the best of mountain-town convenience. Walking-distance homes in Woodland Heights and the Midland Area range from renovated cottages to spacious family properties, many with Pikes Peak views. The downtown location means you are close to restaurants, the weekly farmers market, and community events that give Woodland Park its strong sense of place.

For coffee lovers who want their daily ritual to be a five-minute walk rather than a twenty-minute drive, this stretch of Midland Avenue is one of the best places to own a home in the entire Ute Pass corridor.
