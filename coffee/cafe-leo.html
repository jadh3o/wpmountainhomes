Cafe Leo

Woodland Park's early-morning-to-evening cafe serving artisan espresso, teas, and a full selection of sweet treats on the west end of Midland Avenue.

About Cafe Leo

Cafe Leo has established itself as one of Woodland Park's go-to gathering spots, and for good reason. With more than 1,000 online reviews and consistently high ratings, it has earned a reputation for friendly service, quality coffee, and an atmosphere that makes you want to stay a while. Located on the west end of Midland Avenue, it is a convenient stop for residents of the surrounding neighborhoods and a popular destination for visitors passing through on Highway 24.

The cafe has a warm, welcoming vibe that reflects the personality of Woodland Park itself: unpretentious, community-minded, and genuinely inviting. It is the kind of place where the staff remembers your order and the regulars become friends. Whether you are meeting someone for a morning latte, settling in for an afternoon of work, or picking up a treat on the way home, Cafe Leo delivers.

What Makes It Special

What sets Cafe Leo apart from the other excellent coffee shops in Woodland Park is its combination of early hours and late closing time. Opening at 6 AM means it serves the before-work crowd and early-morning hikers heading to Pikes Peak or the Crags trailhead. Staying open until 8 PM means it is still there when you want a post-dinner coffee or a quiet evening retreat. Most coffee shops in town close by 5, making Cafe Leo's extended hours a real advantage.

The menu spans a full range of espresso-based drinks, drip coffees, teas, and an impressive bakery case stocked with sweet treats. Gluten-free options are available, and the baked goods rotate seasonally. The staff is consistently praised for their warmth and energy, which keeps customers coming back even when other options are just as close.

Popular Menu Items

Address & Directions

Cafe Leo is located at 320 W Midland Ave, Woodland Park, CO 80863, on the west side of the downtown strip. Parking is available along Midland Avenue and in the adjacent lot. From Highway 24, head south on any of the downtown cross streets and turn west on Midland. Phone: (719) 687-6670.

Living Near Cafe Leo

The west end of Midland Avenue borders some of Woodland Park's most sought-after neighborhoods. Properties in nearby Sunnywood Manor and the Tamarac area offer mature trees, larger lots, and the kind of quiet mountain setting that draws buyers to this part of Colorado. Homes here are typically priced between the mid-$400s and the upper $600s, with a mix of updated family homes and original mountain cabins.

Being close to Cafe Leo also means you are close to the rest of downtown: groceries, the library, Memorial Park, community events, and an easy on-ramp to Highway 24 for commutes to Colorado Springs. It is the kind of daily convenience that makes mountain living practical, not just scenic.
