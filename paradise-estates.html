Paradise Estates

One of Woodland Park's largest and most established subdivisions, offering diverse home styles and easy access to Highway 24.

About Paradise Estates

Situated on the southeastern edge of Woodland Park, Paradise Estates is one of the city's most well-known and established neighborhoods. With over 250 homes built between the 1980s and 2010s, the subdivision offers a wide range of architectural styles — from classic ranch homes to bi-level and contemporary mountain designs.

The neighborhood benefits from excellent proximity to major thoroughfares, making the commute to Colorado Springs straightforward. Residents enjoy the convenience of being close to Woodland Park's downtown shops, restaurants, and services while maintaining a residential mountain feel.

What Makes Paradise Estates Special

Paradise Estates sits at the gateway between Woodland Park and the Pike National Forest, giving residents immediate access to hiking, mountain biking, and outdoor recreation. Many lots back up to wooded areas, offering privacy and wildlife sightings — deer, elk, and wild turkey are common visitors.

The newer section of the neighborhood, known as Haven at Paradise, features luxury homes surrounding Pikes National Forest with modern finishes, larger lots, and custom builds that command premium prices.

Schools & Amenities

Paradise Estates is served by the Woodland Park School District RE-2, which includes Gateway Elementary, Summit Elementary, Woodland Park Middle School, and Woodland Park High School. The neighborhood is a short drive from City Market, Pikes Peak Regional Hospital, and the downtown commercial district along Highway 24.

Who Lives Here

Paradise Estates attracts a mix of families, commuters working in Colorado Springs, remote workers, and retirees drawn to the mountain lifestyle. The diverse housing stock means there are options for first-time buyers, move-up buyers, and those looking for custom mountain living.
